Why Every Hispanic Adores Selena

When you hear about this inspiring woman the first thing to mind is her music! Selena Quintanilla was a very successful women not to mention tremendously gorgeous! Her career started just like everyone else's, at rock bottom. She first start recording professionally in 1981. Tejan music was more a male music gender and so people would mistreat her for what she loved doing. Venues around Texas would shut her and out therefor she wasn't able to preform. Selena was so idepentent and didn't give up until she had the ability to do what she desired most. Later when her amazing talent was finally discovered people started going crazy and had loads of affection towards her. Especially After winning the Tejan Music Award her career had blown off. In her time she had won various awards, started her own fashion line boutique, and became one of the the best female artists in Latin music history. Sadly with deep sorrow her friend and former mananger, shot Selena Quintanilla. George W. Bush decalred that Selena's birthday as Selena Day in Texas. That's how big and important of a role model, she meant to people. To this day the Queen lives on in our hearts and through her music. Selena may not be here but she lives on forever.

Timeline of Selena Quintanilla

  • 1971, April 16, born in Lake Jackson, Texas
  • 1974, Selena's dad started a band, "Selena y los Dinos" which included Selena's siblings, Suzette and A.B.
  • 1975, Selena makes her first debut in her dad's restaurant, "Papa Gayos"
  • 1985, The goups effort paid off when 14 year old Selena recorded her first album for a record company.
  • 1987, At just 15 years old Selena won the best female vocalist at the tejano music awards
  • 1989, Selena was signed by EMI latin records
  • 1990, Selena's album, "Ven Conmigo" was released and it was the first Tejano album written by a female singer to win golds
  • 1992, On April 2, Selena marries her boyfriend Chris Perez
  • 1193, Selena launches her first clothing line business and she appears in the Mexican Soap Opera, "Dos mujeres, un camino"
  • 1994, Selena won her first grammy
  • 1997, On March 31, Selena lost her life due to loosing a lot of blood from getting shot
  • 1997, On March 21, the movie "Selena" was released to honor her
